Uniq is an app for iOS that brings the college researching and application process into one unified app. It allows high school students to explore and compare schools and programs within seconds. Uniq will use your information and adapt to what you value most in a school or program to provide personal recommendations, notifications, and deadlines alerts to guide you through the application process. With Uniq, students can significantly improve the quality of their college research while using less time than traditional methods.
